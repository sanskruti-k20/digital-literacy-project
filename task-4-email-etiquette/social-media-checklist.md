# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

## ✅ Do’s
- Maintain a professional and clean profile
- Think before posting any content
- Use privacy settings to protect your data
- Share informative and useful content
- Respect others' opinions online

## ❌ Don’ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive information
- Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
- Do not spread fake news or misinformation
- Avoid engaging in online arguments
- Do not misuse someone else's data or images
