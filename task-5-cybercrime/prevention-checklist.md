# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## General Safety Tips
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
- Always verify website URLs before entering information
- Never share OTP, passwords, or personal details with anyone
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Keep your apps and devices updated regularly
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities
- Check sender email addresses carefully before responding

## UPI / Financial Safety Tips
- Never approve unknown payment requests
- Always double-check UPI ID and recipient details before sending money

## Report Cybercrime
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Helpline Number: 1930
